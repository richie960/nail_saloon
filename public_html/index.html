About Studio

Once nestled in a quaint spa, Jessy Nails, founded by the visionary CEO Jesse Kinoti , bloomed into a renowned nail studio. As Jesse's reputation as a public figure soared, so did the studio's popularity. Daily influxes of eager customers sought the studio's exceptional nail services, transforming it into a bustling hub for beauty enthusiasts. The journey from a humble spa to a celebrated and expansive nail haven reflects not just a business's success but a tale of passion, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Jesse Kinoti, the ingenious mind behind Jessy Nails, began the journey with a small team in the cozy spa, driven by a passion for perfecting the art of nail care. With a keen eye for trends and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction, Jessy transformed the studio into a beacon of elegance and style. The studio's growth mirrored Jessy's own rise as a public figure, attracting not just local clients but a nationwide following. Today, Jessy Nails stands tall as a symbol of success, where the fusion of talent, dedication, and trendsetting beauty services has created an iconic destination cherished by clients from all walks of life.
